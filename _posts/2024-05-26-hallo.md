Hello there,

Thank you for stopping by. I'm **Jishen Pfeiffer** — a medical doctor by training and a Digital Health researcher by passion. You can learn more about my background on the [About](/about/) page.

This blog is where I think out loud about the questions that keep me busy:

- How are digital technologies reshaping the future of healthcare?
- What does the state of the art in Digital Health actually look like — beyond the hype?
- Which critical and unmet needs in healthcare can technology genuinely address?
- How do we drive tech-enabled, patient-centric medical practice at scale?
- What are the real barriers to adoption — technical, clinical, cultural?
- What does the research journey of a PhD student in Digital Health look like?

I write about **medical AI**, **health data standards** (HL7 FHIR, OpenEHR), **interoperability**, and the messy, exciting reality of bringing technology into clinical practice.

Explore my articles, notes from the research frontier, and honest takes on where digital health is heading.
